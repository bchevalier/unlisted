# Subscription & Entitlements

## Objective
Monetize Knokio Direct via subscription.

## Scope
- Free vs paid plans
- Stripe integration
- Feature gating

## Constraints
- No usage-based billing
- No paid knocks in V1
- Stripe as source of truth

## Deliverables
- Checkout flow
- Webhooks
- Entitlement checks

## Acceptance Criteria
- Paid users get higher limits
- Downgrades enforced correctly
- Billing is self-service
