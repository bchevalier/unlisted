# Abuse Prevention & Safety

## Objective
Prevent spam and misuse with minimal friction.

## Scope
- Rate limiting
- Blocklists
- Abuse reports
- CAPTCHA

## Constraints
- No AI moderation
- No heavy heuristics

## Deliverables
- Enforcement logic
- Report storage

## Acceptance Criteria
- Spam reduced
- Legitimate users unaffected
- Admins can intervene
